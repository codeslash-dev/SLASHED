FAQ

Questions I get asked a lot

What technologies do you use?
I work exclusively in WordPress with clean, custom PHP — no Elementor, no Divi, no page-builder bloat. On the front end I use modern CSS and vanilla JS where needed.
How long does a typical project take?
Most builds take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. A focused 5-page site can launch in 3–4 weeks.
What happens after launch?
You either transition to a Care Plan for ongoing updates, monitoring, and support — or receive a full handoff document.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. Standard structure is: 40% upfront at kickoff, 40% at design sign-off, 20% at launch.
