FAQ

Questions I get asked a lot

What technologies do you use?
I work exclusively in WordPress with clean, custom PHP — no Elementor, no Divi, no page-builder bloat. On the front end I use modern CSS and vanilla JS where needed. WooCommerce for e-commerce, ACF for content management.
How long does a typical project take?
Most builds take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. A focused 5-page site can launch in 3–4 weeks; a full WooCommerce build typically takes 8–12 weeks. Timeline is locked in before work begins — it's part of the gate system.
Do you work with clients outside of Europe?
Yes — around 40% of my clients are in North America and beyond. Communication is entirely async-friendly. I'm in Central European time (UTC+1/+2) which gives good overlap with both US and Asia-Pacific teams.
What if I don't have content ready?
We can discuss a phased approach where design begins with placeholder content, but I'll be honest: sites that launch with proper copy and imagery consistently outperform those that don't. I can recommend good copywriters if needed.
Can you redesign my existing WordPress site?
Yes. For redesigns, I assess the existing codebase first and advise whether to rebuild from scratch or optimise the current structure — whichever is most cost-effective for your situation. I'll always be honest about the trade-offs.
What happens after launch?
You either transition to a Care Plan (recommended) for ongoing updates, monitoring, and support — or receive a full handoff document. Care plan clients get a developer who already knows their site, not a helpdesk ticket system.
How do Care Plans work?
Care Plans are monthly retainers that cover updates, backups, uptime monitoring, security scanning, and a set number of support/edit hours. You pay one fixed amount per month and your site is looked after proactively — not reactively.
What is included in the project price?
Fixed-price projects include: discovery, design (2 revision rounds), development, QA, and launch. Hosting, third-party plugins, and stock imagery are not included but are discussed upfront — no surprise costs at invoice time.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. Standard structure is: 40% upfront at kickoff, 40% at design sign-off, 20% at launch. For larger projects ($10,000+) a 4-stage payment schedule is available. I don't ask for 100% upfront.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
Each design phase includes two structured rounds of feedback. After sign-off, changes outside the agreed scope are treated as addendum work. The gate system exists precisely to prevent "I don't like it" moments at development stage.
Why choose a solo developer over an agency?
You get direct access to the person doing the work — not a sales rep, not a project manager, not a junior developer. The quality is consistent because I personally do every project. The cost is lower because there's no agency overhead.
What is the minimum budget to work together?
For custom builds: $4,000. For optimisation/performance audits: $1,500. For Care Plans: $149/month. If your budget is below these thresholds, I'm happy to point you towards good alternatives rather than waste either of our time.
